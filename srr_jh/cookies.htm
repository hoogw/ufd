YOUR BROWSER SETTINGS DOES NOT ACCEPT COOKIES.

COOKIES ARE VERY SMALL TEXT FILES.

THEY CANNOT CAUSE ANY HARM TO YOUR COMPUTER.

THEY ALLOW OUR APPLICATION TO ESTABLISH A UNIQUE CONNECTION TO COMMUNICATE WITH YOUR BROWSER.


IF YOU ARE USING INTERNET EXPLORER:

GO TO "TOOLS" >> "INTERNET OPTIONS" >> "SECURITY" THEN SET YOU SECURITY LEVEL TO THE DEFAULT LEVEL.

ALSO,

SELECT THE TAB "PRIVACY" AND SET THE LEVEL TO DEFAULT.

THIS SHOULD ALLOW YOUR BROWSER TO WORK WITH OUR SITE.

IF YOU ARE USING NETSCAPE:

COOKIES SHOULD BE UNDER PREFERENCES, ADVANCED

SET THE COOKIES OPTIONS TO ACCEPT ALL COOKIES.


OTHER BROWSERS:

FIND OUT WHERE YOUR COOKIES SETTINGS ARE LOCATED AND MAKE SURE ALL COOKIES ARE ACCEPTED.
