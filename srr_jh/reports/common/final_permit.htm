WARNING

No person shall open or remove the cover of any maintenance hole, or allow any maintenance hole to remain open in or upon any street or sidewalk without setting barriers in accordance with the latest edition of the Work Area Control Manual.  Guards or Warning devices shall be maintained at all times while the maintenance hole remains open.  No traffic lane shall be obstructed in any manner in major or secondry highways between the hours of 6:00 a.am. and 9:00 a.m. or 3:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. without special written permission. Only one traffic lane may be obstructed at other times.   This permit is not valid for work in all streets affected by "Holiday Season Contruction Moratorium" between November 15 and the following January 2 of each year.

DISCLAIMER ON MAINTENANCE HOLE (MH) OPENING PERMIT CARD This grants permission for opening of City of Los Angeles sanitary sewer and storm drain maintenance holes only.  Opening of maintenance holes owned by any other agency requires permission from that agency. This does not allow applicant to enter said maintenance hole(s).  Phyical entry into maintenance holes requires permission from the City of Los Angeles, Department of Public Works, Bureau of Sanitation, Wastewater Collection Systems Division (323) 342-6006.   Please be advised that personel entering maintenance holes should be property trained and equipped for confined space entry per Cal/OSHA Title 8, Section 5157.
